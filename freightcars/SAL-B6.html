SAL B-6 Boxcar

1 Car

Built from April of 1937, the Seaboard Air Line's 18000-series B-6 boxcars were brand-new when one arrived at Oreminea with one of the final shipments featured here. Seaboard's B-6 boxcars were 1932 A.R.A. design steel cars, one of the first standard boxcar designs adopted by railroads across the country.
