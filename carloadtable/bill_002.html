1925-02-24 B&O 193229 M-15a Fate-Root-Heath Co. (Plymouth Locomotive Works) Woodbury Clay Co. 1 Gasoline Locomotive (15500 lbs.) Crammed into a boxcar for its journey from Ohio, this "gasoline locomotive" was a small narrow-gauge 4-wheel industrial locomotive built by the Plymouth Locomotive Works (Fate-Root-Heath), a 7-ton Model DLB, for hauling mine cars around the clay pits. A photo of it in operation can be seen here, courtesy of Penn State University Library's Special Collections.
