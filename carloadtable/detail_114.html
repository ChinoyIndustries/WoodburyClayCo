1925-02-24 B&O 193229 M15a Fate-Root-Heath Co., Plymouth OH WCC 1 Gasoline Locomotive (15500 lbs.) Shipped in a B&O M-15a furniture boxcar, this "gasoline locomotive" was a small 4-wheel industrial locomotive built by the Plymouth Locomotive Works (Fate-Root-Heath), a 7-ton Model DLB, for use on narrow gauge trackage within the clay mines. A photo of it in operation can be seen here, courtesy of Penn State University Library's Special Collections.
