Welcome!

Welcome to the Woodbury Clay Co. project, where we take a stack of random paperwork from 90 years ago and shuffle it over and over until it turns into a website!

Oh man, it still looks like the 90s in here. Check back as often as you like though, things are going up around here as fast as I can bolt 'em together! The priority is on still getting all the features built, so don't expect shiny graphics and polish just yet. I'm well aware of how bad my CSS is.

Whether you're Dr. B, one of my fellow Pitt Digital-Humanities students here to see what I've been working on, or one of my railfan friends who's decided to come take a look at what all the complaining has been about, head on over to the Introduction to learn more about what's here so far!
