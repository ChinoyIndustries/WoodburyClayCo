Mingo Works (Carnegie Steel)

Mingo Junction, OH

5 Shipments

Founded as the Mingo Iron Works in 1869, etc etc. The plant was located at the junction of the Wheeling & Lake Erie and the PCC&St.L (Pittsburgh, Cincinnati, Chicago & St. Louis Railway), which was absorbed by the PRR as its "Panhandle Route" in 1921, and continues to operate as part of Norfolk Southern today. The Mingo Works was sold by Carnegie-Illinois to Wheeling Steel in 1945, and yada yada yada shut down in year. In 2018, the idled mill was purchased and revived by JSW Steel, a multinational steel company based in India.
