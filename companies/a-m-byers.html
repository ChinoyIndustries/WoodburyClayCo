A. M. Byers Co.

Economy, PA (Ambridge)

1 Shipment

This area just south of Conway Yard along the Ohio river, formerly known as Economy, is now a part of Ambridge, PA. Talk about what this company manufactured. Though demolished otherwise, the plant's smokestacks still stand, emblazoned with the "A. M. Byers Co." name, and are tagged as a landmark on Google Maps.
