Canadian Porcelain Co.

Hamilton, Ontario

1 Shipment

on TH&B at Aberdeen yard. Plant bought out by the <a href="/404.html">Lapp Insulator Co.</a>, who shut it down in 1984 following a strike. Facility subsequently demolished and the site is now occupied by condos.
