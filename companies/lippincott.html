Lippincott & Co.

Altoona, PA

1 Shipment

Lippincott & Co. was a wholesale grocer based in Philadelphia, with a secondary location in Altoona. Along with <a href="/404.html">Comly-Flanigan & Co.</a>, it was one of a number of wholesale grocery concerns owned or operated by Jesse H. Lippincott, one of the 19th-century 'robber barons' involved with the South Fork Fishing and Hunting Club, notorious for its role in causing the Johnstown Flood.
