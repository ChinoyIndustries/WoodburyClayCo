Penn General Supply Co.

2 Shipments

The Pennsylvania General Supply Co. was headquartered at at 103 Market St. in Pittsburgh (not rail-served). Station listed as Pittsburgh 4252, does not correspond to C.T.1000. Point on map is company's street address.
