Gorman-Rupp Co.

Mansfield, OH

2 Shipments

Manufacturer of industrial pumps founded in 1931, still in business. Their factory is located on the former B&O at its junction with the PRR in Mansfield, now abandoned just beyond end of line now operated by the Ashland Rwy.
